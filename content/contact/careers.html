Career

Some jobs are merely jobs; you drag yourself to the office to deal with dreary tasks in order to secure your finances and pay off the bill.

At SKDRDP, we make far more than just money. Every day, we create opportunities for the less privileged to rise above their poverty and to live their dreams without worrying about financial security. Every day, we empower the underprivileged to achieve positive and lasting change. We help our members believe in themselves. We link them with the world and guide them through every difficult step so that they can bring a better future to the next generation. Every day, we bring poverty a little closer to extinction. Our job makes a difference. If you would love to work in a positive and meaningful environment, send your resume today.
