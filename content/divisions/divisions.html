Divisions

SKDRDP being a very large organization with a very grand vision of poverty alleviation and sustainable development of rural folks, has one of the special organization structure to effectively work towards it. SKDRDP has task focused divisions with matrix like organization structure to effectively manage its diverse programs. Some divisions works solely on one important program, where as some other division covers a broad spectrum of programs. The divisions are segregated with the relative importance of task done by them.

Apart from internal divisions SKDRDP also has subsidiary companies which look after very high priority tasks like Insurance, Product sales, Alcohol De-addiction.
