Community Development Programs Division

The Community Development Programs(CDP) division is one of the important division of SKDRDPÂ®.The division was established with the purpose of providing basic amenities to rural populace, as well as uplift them. CDP was established with following objectives.

  1. Providing help to build basic facilities at village areas.
  2. Encouraging villagers and SHG's in rural places to involve in developmental activities.
  3. Providing basic amenities with the help of Panchayat's, Government and Private Institutions.
  4. Providing support to helpful amenities like school building, hospital building, animal clinic, Aanganwadi, Drinking water unit, New connecting roads, sanitation facilities, Youth centres.
  5. Providing support to village level Rural Development Organizations.
